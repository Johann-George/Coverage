Premium Calculation Summary
ℹ️
How Gross Premium is Calculated
Net Risk Premium = (SA ÷ 1,000) × Age-Banded Rate
+ Smoker Loading (if applicable)
+ Occupation Loading (if applicable)
= Risk Premium
+ Admin Loading (12%) + Profit Loading (8%) + Reinsurance Loading (5%) + Cat Loading (2%)
= Gross Premium
− Applicable Discounts
= Final Premium (subject to minimum of KES 100)
